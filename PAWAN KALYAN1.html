PAWAN KALYAN BIO GRAPHY

Pawan Kalyan was born to Venkat Rao and Smt. Anjana Devi in Mogalthur, Andhra Pradesh, India, in 1965. In October of 2007, Pawan established the Common Man Pawan Kalyan's actual name is Konidela Kalyan Babu. He began to call himself as Pawan Kalyan when he worked on his first film, Akkada Abbai Ikkada Ammayi
